Alpha Xi Delta: Delta Lambda Chapter

Welcome to our chapter!

We are one of the oldest greek life organizations on RIT's campus having been here for over 50 years. Our chapter has members from many diverse backgrounds and majors. We currently have a sister in every college at RIT. We strive to grow academically, personally, and professionally to uphold the ideals that our founders set in 1893.

To support each member in every endeavor they seek to take on and appreciate individuals unique skills and perspective is a goal. Our members inspire sisterhood, leadership, and service from each other.
